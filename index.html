About Mercedes

Mercedes (formally Mercedes‑Benz) is one of the world’s oldest and most renowned automobile manufacturers. Known for pioneering safety innovations (airbags, crumple zones), for blending advanced engineering with handcrafted luxury, and for pushing boundaries in electrification and autonomous driving.

History — a timeline

1886
The first automobile
Karl Benz builds the Benz Patent-Motorwagen, widely regarded as the first true automobile.
1926
Mercedes‑Benz formed
Merger of Benz & Cie. and Daimler Motoren Gesellschaft creates the brand we know today.
1950s
Safety innovations
Introduction of safety features and the rise of the S-Class as a benchmark for luxury sedans.
2019+
Electrification & MBUX
Heavy push into electric vehicles (EQ range) and software-driven experiences (MBUX infotainment).
